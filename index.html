About Us

Usus Al-Janoub is a specialized engineering firm based in Basra, Iraq, offering integrated services in design, construction, and project supervision.

Vision

To lead smart and sustainable engineering solutions in Iraq and the region through innovation, quality, and reliability.

Mission

To provide comprehensive, high-quality engineering solutions in design, execution, and supervision, in line with international standards to ensure customer satisfaction and contribute to a modern urban future.
